Welcome to Top Trumps: Greek Mythology

How to play:

Select the attribute you think will beat your opponent, then click the compare cards button to reveal your opponents card. If you win the hand then a point will be added to your score, if you lost that hand then a point will be given to your opponent. If a draw occurs then both players will be given a point.

The person with the highest score wins!
