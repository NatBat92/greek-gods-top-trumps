Welcome to Top Trumps: Greek Mythology

How to play:

Select the attribute you think will beat your opponent, then click the 'Compare' button to reveal your opponents card. If you win the hand, a point will be added to your score, if you lose then a point will be given to your opponent. If a draw occurs, both players will be given a point.

The person with the highest score wins!
