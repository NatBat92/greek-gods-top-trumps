Welcome to Top Trumps: Greek Mythology

How to play:

  1. Press the New Game button to start the game.
  2. Each player will be given 10 random cards.
  3. Select the attribute you think will beat your opponent, then click the 'Compare' button to reveal your opponents card.
  4. If you win the hand, a point will be added to your score, if you lose then a point will be given to your opponent.
  5. If a draw occurs, both players will be given a point.
  6. There will be 10 rounds, at the end of the 10th round, the person with the highest score wins!
